📢 Patch 1.0.1 Has Arrived!

Hello everyone! 👋🏽
We’re excited to announce that **Rhythms and Drive – Patch 1.0.1** is now live! 🚗💨
This minor update focuses on improving clarity, gameplay balance, and visual polish.
✨ What’s New?
- ℹ️ Information Button – a new popup button has been added beside Mute, giving players quick access to the latest news, updates, and important game details directly in-game.
- 🎨 Title Card Update – the title card now features a bold red color with animated effects, symbolizing intensity and making the intro more visually striking.
- 🌐 Language Polish – all text has been standardized to English for consistency, while a few small popups remain in Bahasa Melayu as fun easter eggs.
- ⚙️ Gameplay Balance – adjusted tempo level, obstacle spawn rate, and level speed progression to create a smoother difficulty curve and more enjoyable driving experience.
Thank you for your continued support and feedback — every idea helps us keep improving **Rhythms and Drive**. 💖
Stay tuned for more updates, and happy driving with rhythm! 🚗🎶

- Gagakz Studio 🐦‍⬛
